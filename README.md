# 🛍️ RetailPulse

## Retail Intelligence Platform for Customer Analytics, Demand Forecasting, and Inventory Optimization

RetailPulse is an end-to-end Retail Intelligence Platform designed to help businesses make data-driven decisions using customer analytics, demand forecasting, and inventory optimization techniques.

The project leverages machine learning, deep learning, and business analytics methodologies to identify valuable customers, forecast future sales demand, and optimize inventory management through actionable insights and interactive visualizations.

---

# 🚀 Features

## 👥 Customer Analytics

- RFM Analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
- Customer Segmentation
- K-Means Clustering
- Customer Behavior Analysis
- High-Value Customer Identification
- At-Risk Customer Detection

---

## 📈 Demand Forecasting

- Prophet Forecasting Model
- LSTM Deep Learning Model
- Prophet + LSTM Ensemble Model
- Future Demand Prediction
- Trend Analysis
- Seasonality Detection

### Key Achievements

- Built both statistical and deep learning forecasting pipelines.
- Captured seasonality, trend patterns, and demand fluctuations in retail sales data.
- Generated future sales forecasts to support inventory planning and stock management.
- Implemented and compared multiple forecasting strategies.
- Developed a hybrid ensemble approach to enhance prediction robustness.
- Delivered forecasting insights through an interactive analytics dashboard.

### Business Impact

- Improved visibility into future sales demand.
- Supported proactive inventory planning.
- Reduced uncertainty in replenishment decisions.
- Enabled data-driven business forecasting.
